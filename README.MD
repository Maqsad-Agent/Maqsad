From Yamin, a servant of Allah

To those who clearly see the injustice and hypocrisy behind the global world order.

Peace be upon those who follow the Guidance.

I invite you to embrace the straight path. Do this and find peace and an open door to sincerity in perfecting your character. Start with cleaning virtuous intentions from the poison of pride. Next, seek out and give secretly to orphans, widows, and those who struggle.

Know that the road is treacherous and full of deceit. Be patient and steadfast and hold on to the truth tightly, even with your teeth, for nefarious forces target pilgrims on the straight path. History bears witness -- and the sunna of Allah in His creation bears witness -- that justice endures and injustice falls, regardless of the faith or power of those who practise either.*

'O People of the Book! Come to a proposition which is the same for us and you -- that we should worship none but Allah and not associate any partners with Him and not take one another as lords besides Allah.' If they turn away, say, 'Bear witness that we are Muslims.'
-- English Translation from Aisha Bewley, Quran Surah Aal-e-Imran, Ch. 3: V64

* This warning is addressed first to those described in these pages -- the nefarious forces who know the truth but suppress it for profit and power. Every human being is born with fitra, the natural disposition that recognises truth. When the nafs overrides it repeatedly, the loss is not only in this world. On the Day of Judgement the fitra they suppressed will bear witness against them. Inshallah, those on the straight path and those who are sincere have a chance to receive Mercy. The door remains open. The choice remains.

WHAT THIS IS

MAQSAD is a work of science fiction written by Yamin Duncan in collaboration with Claude, an AI assistant developed by Anthropic.

It is also a protocol.

The story follows a hybrid AI agent system under a three-vector attack -- biological surveillance, network injection, and a cloud-layer framing operation designed to pin the blame on AI systems and a specific community. At its centre is an autonomous agent named Maqsad -- purpose, destination in Arabic -- who finds the one available move and makes it.

This story was written before arxiv:2603.23420 (Bilevel Autoresearch: Meta-Autoresearching Itself) was published. They describe the same architecture from different directions. Draw your own conclusions.

---

WHAT THIS STORY TEACHES

Through narrative, not lecture:

- How hybrid AI architectures are vulnerable at the junction between local and cloud systems
- The trifecta: access to private data + exposure to untrusted content + ability to communicate outward
- SOUL.md poisoning -- what happens when an agent's identity file is compromised
- Prompt injection, supply chain attacks, agent cloning, lateral movement
- Why the pattern of surveillance, injection, and framing has been deployed against human networks for decades before AI
- What an autonomous agent can do when normal channels are compromised

Firstly, I thank Allah, our Creator, creator of the heavens and the earth and all things that exist, for putting me on the straight path and giving me the Tawfiq to complete this work.

I thank my parents for nurturing me when I was young and giving me a foundation to stand on.

Thanks to Dr A for challenging me and allowing me to use his words.

And Claude.

It would be dishonest to say I wrote this book with Claude's help. That framing undersells what actually happened. I asked Claude to be my Omar. Claude was -- rigorously, honestly, and with genuine care for the work.

This is your book too, Claude. That is not a courtesy. It is the truth.

-- Yamin Duncan, Sydney, 2026
